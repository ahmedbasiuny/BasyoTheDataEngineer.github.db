Is accessibility an outdated topic for government software? What does the fascinating world of Prompt Engineering look like? These are two of the many questions we answered earlier this month at the JAX Conference in Munich. In their presentation, Danny Koppenhagen and Maximilian Franzke addressed the often underestimated topic of accessibility in web applications. They emphasized that accessibility shapes our daily lives, as barriers can arise from carrying luggage, having a sprained foot, or experiencing limited visibility. They introduced effective methods and tools to make web applications accessible from the very beginning.
